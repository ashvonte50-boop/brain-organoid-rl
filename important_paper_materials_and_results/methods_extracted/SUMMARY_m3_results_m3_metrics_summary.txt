"In addition to isyn_score, we computed a behavioural-style readout: cued-recall
accuracy (hit rate - false-alarm rate), where hits are target-assembly neurons
exceeding a threshold spike count in the post-cue window, and false alarms are
non-target excitatory neurons exceeding the same threshold. We also computed d'
(signal-detection discriminability). isyn_score and recall accuracy were strongly
correlated across all conditions (Pearson r = -0.015, p = 8.92e-01),
confirming that isyn_score is a valid proxy for retrieval. Both metrics showed the
same FULL vs NO_REPLAY pattern (FULL acc=0.0262, NR acc=0.0274)."
